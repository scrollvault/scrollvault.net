About

Welcome to ScrollVault — your daily source for Magic: The Gathering news, competitive strategy, deck guides, spoiler breakdowns, and set reviews.

What We Cover

  • Breaking News — Ban list updates, tournament results, Wizards announcements, and Arena patch notes
  • Strategy — Format guides, metagame analysis, draft walkthroughs, and matchup breakdowns
  • Deck Guides — Budget builds, competitive lists, and sideboard tech for every format
  • Spoilers — Card reveals, set speculation, and first impressions from upcoming releases
  • Set Reviews — Comprehensive breakdowns of every new set for Constructed and Limited

Interactive Tools

We've built a suite of MTG tools to help you optimize your decks:

  • Top Decks - Competitive decklists across all formats
  • Dual Land Reference Guide - Compare every fetch, shock, check, and fast land
  • Mana Base Calculator - Calculate optimal land counts for your colors
  • Draft Tool - In-progress drafting assistant

Who We Are

We're a team of dedicated Magic players who have been slinging cardboard since the early days. This blog runs on a mix of human passion and AI-assisted research to bring you timely, accurate coverage of everything happening in the world of MTG.

Have a tip, correction, or just want to talk Magic? Drop us a line at support@scrollvault.net.
