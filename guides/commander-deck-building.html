How to Build a Commander Deck — EDH Deck Building Guide

Commander (also known as EDH) is Magic's most popular format, and building your first deck is one of the most rewarding experiences in the game. This guide walks you through every step, from choosing your commander to finalizing your mana base.

The Basics

  • 100 cards including your commander
  • Singleton — only one copy of each card (except basic lands)
  • Color identity — every card must match your commander's color identity
  • Starting life: 40 (21 commander damage from a single commander is lethal)

Step 1: Choose Your Commander

Your commander defines your deck. Look for a legendary creature with an ability that inspires you. Consider:

  • Strategy: Does the commander suggest a clear game plan (tokens, spellslinger, voltron, combo)?
  • Colors: 2-3 color commanders offer the best balance of card access and mana base simplicity
  • Budget: Some commanders thrive with budget cards, while others need expensive staples
  • Popularity: Sites like EDHREC show the most-built commanders and common includes

Step 2: Build Your Mana Base

A reliable mana base is critical in a format with 2+ colors and 100 cards. Start with 36-38 lands:

  • Basics: Always include enough basics for fetch effects and Path to Exile
  • Dual lands: Use shock lands, check lands, and battle lands for fixing (see our Mana Base Guide and Dual Land Cycles)
  • Utility lands: Command Tower, Exotic Orchard, Reliquary Tower
  • Mana rocks: Sol Ring, Arcane Signet, and signets for your colors

Use our Mana Base Calculator to get precise land counts for your color distribution.

Step 3: The 10-10-10 Framework

A balanced Commander deck needs three categories of support cards:

  • 10 Ramp sources: Sol Ring, Arcane Signet, Cultivate, Kodama's Reach, signets, talismans
  • 10 Card draw sources: Rhystic Study, Beast Whisperer, Phyrexian Arena, Harmonize
  • 10 Removal/interaction: Swords to Plowshares, Chaos Warp, Beast Within, Counterspell, board wipes

Step 4: Fill Your Strategy Slots

With ~37 lands, ~10 ramp, ~10 draw, ~10 removal, and your commander, you have about 32 slots for cards that advance your deck's strategy. Focus on cards that synergize with your commander's ability.

Power Level and Brackets

Commander uses a bracket system to help players find games at similar power levels:

  • Bracket 1 (Casual): Precons and lightly upgraded decks. No infinite combos, no fast mana beyond Sol Ring.
  • Bracket 2 (Focused): Clear strategy, some synergy, budget-conscious. Infinite combos allowed if they need 3+ pieces.
  • Bracket 3 (Optimized): Tuned lists with efficient combos and strong interaction. Most organized play falls here.
  • Bracket 4 (cEDH): Fully competitive with fast mana, free counterspells, and efficient win conditions.

Check out our Commander Bracket Calculator to estimate your deck's power level.

Budget Tips

  • Start with a preconstructed deck and upgrade gradually
  • Many staples have budget alternatives (e.g., Night's Whisper instead of Rhystic Study)
  • Invest in mana base first — good lands improve every game
  • Check multiple vendors for the best prices on singles
