Elves

Tier 1 7% of meta 53.5% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

A creature-combo deck that floods the board with mana elves, then uses Distant Melody to refill and Quirion Ranger for repeated untaps. Can generate lethal board states as early as turn 3-4.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$20
Key Cards: Quirion Ranger, Distant Melody, Priest of Titania
