Jund Wildfire

Tier 1 5.59% of meta 52% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Jund Wildfire is a midrange strategy that uses Wildfire to reset the board while generating token advantage through Krark-Clan Shaman and token producers. The deck excels at grinding opponents out with incremental advantage and explosive turns combining Wildfire with token generators to rebuild faster than opponents. It has strong matchups against the format's top decks like Mono-Blue Terror and Grixis Affinity.

Archetype: Midrange
Budget: ~$180
Key Cards: Wildfire, Krark-Clan Shaman, Writhing Chrysalis, Elvish Visionary, Lightning Bolt
