Jund Wildfire

Tier 1 • 5.59% meta • 52% win rate

Jund Wildfire is a midrange strategy that uses Wildfire to reset the board while generating token advantage through Krark-Clan Shaman and token producers. The deck excels at grinding opponents out with incremental advantage and explosive turns combining Wildfire with token generators to rebuild fast
