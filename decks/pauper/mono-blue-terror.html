Mono Blue Terror

Tier 1 9% of meta 55% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

A tempo deck built around Tolarian Terror as a cheap, massive threat backed by free counterspells and cantrips. Fills the graveyard quickly to deploy 5/5s for a single blue mana.

Archetype: Tempo
Budget: ~$30
Key Cards: Tolarian Terror, Counterspell, Thought Scour
