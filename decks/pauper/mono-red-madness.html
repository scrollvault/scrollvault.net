Mono Red Madness

Tier 1 8.5% of meta 54.5% win rate Beginner

Deck Overview

A hyper-aggressive red deck using madness to get value from discarding cards. Fiery Temper and Kitchen Imp provide free tempo while burning the opponent out.

Archetype: Aggro
Budget: ~$15
Key Cards: Monastery Swiftspear, Fiery Temper, Lightning Bolt
