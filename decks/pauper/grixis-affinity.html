Grixis Affinity

Tier 1 6% of meta 54% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

An artifact-synergy aggro deck using affinity to deploy threats for cheap. Myr Enforcer and Frogmite cost zero with enough artifacts, while Atog can sacrifice for a lethal Fling.

Archetype: Aggro
Budget: ~$25
Key Cards: Myr Enforcer, Atog, Deadly Dispute
