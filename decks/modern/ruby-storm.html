Ruby Storm

Tier 1 7.2% of meta 57% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

A mono-red storm combo deck that uses ritual spells to generate mana, then casts Past in Flames to rebuy everything and finish with lethal Grapeshot or Empty the Warrens tokens.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$350
Key Cards: Past in Flames, Grapeshot, Rite of Flame
