Jeskai Blink

Tier 1 17.7% of meta 53.2% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

An ETB-value deck that abuses Ephemerate and Phelia to repeatedly trigger powerful creatures. Surged to become the most-played deck at recent Regional Championships with an impressive win rate.

Archetype: Midrange
Budget: ~$600
Key Cards: Ephemerate, Phelia, Exuberant Shepherd, Phlage, Titan of Fire's Fury
