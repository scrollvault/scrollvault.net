Boros Energy

Tier 1 17.5% of meta 55.8% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

The defining deck of Modern, combining individually powerful creatures with energy synergies. Ocelot Pride and Guide of Souls generate value while Phlage provides late-game resilience via escape.

Archetype: Aggro
Budget: ~$550
Key Cards: Ocelot Pride, Ajani, Nacatl Pariah, Phlage, Titan of Fire's Fury
