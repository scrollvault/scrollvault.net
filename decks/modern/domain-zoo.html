Domain Zoo

Tier 2 6.3% of meta 53% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

A five-color aggressive deck that leverages domain to make creatures like Territorial Kavu and Nishoba Brawler into efficient beaters. Leyline of the Guildpact supercharges Scion of Draco to grant every keyword.

Archetype: Aggro
Budget: ~$500
Key Cards: Scion of Draco, Territorial Kavu, Leyline of the Guildpact
