Modern Metagame

A non-rotating format featuring cards from 8th Edition forward.

Amulet Titan

Tier 2 • 5.3% meta • 53% win rate

Use Amulet of Vigor with bounce lands to generate explosive mana, then resolve Primeval Titan to tutor game-ending land combinations. Can kill as early as turn 3.

Boros Energy

Tier 1 • 17.5% meta • 55.8% win rate

The defining deck of Modern, combining individually powerful creatures with energy synergies. Ocelot Pride and Guide of Souls generate value while Phlage provides late-game resilience via escape.

Domain Zoo

Tier 2 • 6.3% meta • 53% win rate

A five-color aggressive deck that leverages domain to make creatures like Territorial Kavu and Nishoba Brawler into efficient beaters. Leyline of the Guildpact supercharges Scion of Draco to grant every keyword.

Izzet Prowess

Tier 2 • 5% meta • 54% win rate

A lean aggro-tempo deck featuring cheap prowess creatures backed by cantrips and free spells. Cori-Steel Cutter from Tarkir: Dragonstorm generates free board presence every turn you cast two spells.

Jeskai Blink

Tier 1 • 17.7% meta • 53.2% win rate

An ETB-value deck that abuses Ephemerate and Phelia to repeatedly trigger powerful creatures. Surged to become the most-played deck at recent Regional Championships with an impressive win rate.

Ruby Storm

Tier 1 • 7.2% meta • 57% win rate

A mono-red storm combo deck that uses ritual spells to generate mana, then casts Past in Flames to rebuy everything and finish with lethal Grapeshot or Empty the Warrens tokens.
