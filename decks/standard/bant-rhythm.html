Bant Rhythm

Tier 1 15% of meta 58.5% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Bant Rhythm combines explosive mana acceleration with toolbox finishers. The deck uses Llanowar Elves, Gene Pollinator, Badgermole Cub, and Spider Manifestation to ramp into Nature's Rhythm, which can fetch game-ending threats like Craterhoof Behemoth or generate overwhelming board presence. The white splash adds Brightglass Gearhulk as a toolbox creature that fetches critical one-mana interactive spells like Seam Rip, Meltstrider's Resolve, and Soul-Guide Lantern, while Formidable Speaker provides discard synergy and tutoring for superior pieces.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$425
Key Cards: Nature's Rhythm, Badgermole Cub, Brightglass Gearhulk, Formidable Speaker, Craterhoof Behemoth, Quantum Riddler
