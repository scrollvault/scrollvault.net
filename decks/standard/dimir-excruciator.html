Dimir Excruciator

Tier 1 5% of meta 58% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

A combo-control deck that fills the graveyard, resolves Doomsday Excruciator to exile the opponent's library, then uses Insatiable Avarice to force lethal draws. Won Pro Tour Lorwyn Eclipsed.

Archetype: Combo
