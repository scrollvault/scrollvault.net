Izzet Spellementals

Tier 1 4.9% of meta 70.7% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

Izzet Spellementals is a breakout deck that leverages Sunderflock's board reset power combined with Eddymurk Crab and Hearth Elemental to dominate the midgame. The deck floods the board with cheap instants and sorceries to fuel its graveyard while using Spell Snare and Burst Lightning to control the early game, then finishes with a cost-reduced Sunderflock that resets the opponent's entire board.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$185
Key Cards: Sunderflock, Eddymurk Crab, Spell Snare, Hearth Elemental, Winternight Stories
