Golgari Midrange

Tier 1 7% of meta 53.5% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

A value-oriented midrange deck leveraging efficient removal and recursive threats. Unholy Annex provides card advantage while Mosswood Dreadknight offers persistent pressure.

Archetype: Midrange
Budget: ~$300
