Sultai Reanimator

Tier 2 6.7% of meta 52% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Fill the graveyard with powerful creatures using looting and self-mill, then resolve Bringer of the Last Gift to reanimate everything for a game-ending attack. Struggled at PT due to heavy graveyard hate.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$320
