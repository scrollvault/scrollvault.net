Jeskai Control

Tier 1 8% of meta 55% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Stabilize with removal and board wipes, counter key threats, and grind out wins with value creatures. Strong against creature-based strategies, especially Simic Rhythm. Made Top 4 at Pro Tour Lorwyn Eclipsed.
