Simic Nature's Rhythm

Tier 1 17.3% of meta 56.5% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

Flood the board with cheap mana creatures, use Badgermole Cub's mana doubling to deploy Ouroboroid as early as turn 3 and overwhelm opponents. The most popular archetype at Pro Tour Lorwyn Eclipsed.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$280
Key Cards: Ouroboroid, Badgermole Cub, Nature's Rhythm
