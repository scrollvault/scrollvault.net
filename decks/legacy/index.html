Legacy Metagame

An eternal format allowing nearly all printed cards with a ban list.

Dimir Tempo

Tier 1 • 6.6% meta • 53% win rate

A black-based tempo deck combining discard spells with Murktide Regent and free countermagic. Trades Wasteland synergies for targeted disruption via Thoughtseize.

Doomsday

Tier 1 • 9.4% meta • 55% win rate

Resolves the card Doomsday to stack a lethal five-card pile, then draws through it to win, typically with Thassa's Oracle. The most powerful combo deck in Legacy when it resolves its namesake card.

Eldrazi Aggro

Tier 1 • 6.8% meta • 54% win rate

Leverages Sol lands and Eldrazi Temple to deploy large colorless threats ahead of curve. Chalice of the Void on one shuts down cantrip-heavy Legacy decks.

Oops All Spells

Tier 2 • 5.6% meta • 52% win rate

A turbo combo deck with zero lands in the deck. Uses Chrome Mox, Lotus Petal, and Elvish Spirit Guide for mana, then mills the entire library for a Thassa's Oracle win or Goblin Charbelcher activation.

Painter

Tier 2 • 5% meta • 53.5% win rate

Combines Painter's Servant with Grindstone for an instant mill combo. Red Elemental Blast becomes a universal counter with Painter naming blue. Imperial Recruiter tutors for combo pieces.

UR Tempo

Tier 1 • 10.3% meta • 54.5% win rate

The premier tempo strategy in Legacy, combining cheap threats like Delver of Secrets with free countermagic. Murktide Regent provides a massive evasive finisher fueled by the filled graveyard.
