Doomsday

Tier 1 9.4% of meta 55% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Resolves the card Doomsday to stack a lethal five-card pile, then draws through it to win, typically with Thassa's Oracle. The most powerful combo deck in Legacy when it resolves its namesake card.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$1500
Key Cards: Doomsday, Thassa's Oracle, Dark Ritual
