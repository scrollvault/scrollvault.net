RogSi

Tier 1 3.7% of meta 27% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

A streamlined two-color combo deck using Rograkh and Silas Renn. Focuses on fast Ad Nauseam into Thassa's Oracle wins with minimal colors for a consistent mana base.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$2500
Key Cards: Rograkh, Son of Rohgahh, Silas Renn, Seeker Adept, Ad Nauseam
