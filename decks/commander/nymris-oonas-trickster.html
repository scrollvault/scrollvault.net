Nymris, Oona's Trickster

Tier 1 2% of meta 57% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

This Nymris, Oona's Trickster deck is a high-powered cEDH control list that leverages the commander's flash and card selection to generate advantage and counter opposing threats. It aims to control the early game with permission and win via Thassa's Oracle combos or by grinding opponents out with card advantage. The deck includes a dense suite of instants and counterspells, a full complement of artifact mana accelerators, and a few win conditions like Notion Thief and Opposition Agent to exploit drawn cards. It is a complex deck requiring tight play and deep knowledge of the meta.

Archetype: Control/Midrange
Budget: ~$4500
Key Cards: Thassa's Oracle, Demonic Consultation, Force of Will
