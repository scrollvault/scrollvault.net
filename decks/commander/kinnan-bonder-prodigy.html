Kinnan, Bonder Prodigy

Tier 1 2.5% of meta 26% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

A fast mana combo deck that uses Kinnan's ability to double mana rock output and his activated ability to cheat expensive creatures into play. Can combo as early as turn 2 with the right hand.

Archetype: Combo
Budget: ~$2000
Key Cards: Kinnan, Bonder Prodigy, Basalt Monolith, Thrasios, Triton Hero
