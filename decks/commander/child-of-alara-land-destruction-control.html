Child of Alara

Tier 1 2% of meta 75% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

This competitive Child of Alara control deck leverages the commander's board wipe ability to reset the game state repeatedly. The strategy involves sacrificing Child of Alara through various outlets to destroy all nonland permanents, then rebuilding with card advantage and gate synergy. Maze's End serves as a resilient win condition that's difficult to interact with, while gates provide both mana fixing and a path to victory. The deck includes multiple board wipes and recursive elements to maintain board dominance throughout the game.

Archetype: Land Destruction/Control
Budget: ~$325
Key Cards: Child of Alara, Maze's End, Baldur's Gate, Phyrexian Tower, Miren, the Moaning Well, Malakir Rebirth, Ondu Inversion
