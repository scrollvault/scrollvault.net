Atraxa, Praetors' Voice

Tier 1 3% of meta 75% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

A competitive Atraxa deck leveraging proliferate to rapidly advance +1/+1 counters and poison counters. The deck combines infect creatures like Blighted Agent with engines such as Ezuri and Inexorable Tide, backed by powerful planeswalkers for resilience. It features high-quality interaction, ramping artifacts, and a five-color mana base optimized for consistent casting.

Archetype: Proliferate
Budget: ~$1800
Key Cards: Atraxa, Praetors' Voice, Ezuri, Stalker of Spheres, Ugin, the Spirit Dragon, Blighted Agent, Rhystic Study
