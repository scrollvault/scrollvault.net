Pioneer Metagame

A non-rotating format featuring cards from Return to Ravnica forward.

Arclight Phoenix

Tier 1 • 8.9% meta • 53% win rate

Cast three spells in a turn to bring back Arclight Phoenix from the graveyard for free. Treasure Cruise provides delve-powered card advantage while Galvanic Iteration can copy Temporal Trespass for combo kills.

Azorius Control

Tier 1 • 11.4% meta • 53.5% win rate

Classic draw-go control that stabilizes with Supreme Verdict and grinds out wins with Teferi and Shark Typhoon. Strong against combo strategies like Greasefang and Lotus Field.

Greasefang Parhelion

Tier 2 • 6.3% meta • 52% win rate

Self-mill creatures dump Parhelion II into the graveyard, then Greasefang reanimates it for a devastating turn 3 attack with 13 power in the air. Glass cannon but can steal games against unprepared opponents.

Gruul Aggro

Tier 2 • 4.6% meta • 51.5% win rate

Pure linear aggro using Burning-Tree Emissary for explosive starts and Embercleave as a lethal finisher. Punishes stumbles and slow draws from any opponent.
