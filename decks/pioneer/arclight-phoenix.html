Arclight Phoenix

Tier 1 8.9% of meta 53% win rate Advanced

Deck Overview

Cast three spells in a turn to bring back Arclight Phoenix from the graveyard for free. Treasure Cruise provides delve-powered card advantage while Galvanic Iteration can copy Temporal Trespass for combo kills.

Archetype: Tempo
Budget: ~$280
Key Cards: Arclight Phoenix, Treasure Cruise, Ledger Shredder
