Rakdos Demons

Tier 1 16% of meta 53% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

Rakdos Demons is a midrange-combo deck that leverages Unholy Annex to generate card advantage and threatening 6/6 tokens. The deck combines efficient disruption (Thoughtseize, Fatal Push) with proactive creatures like Archfiend of the Dross and synergy pieces like Fable of the Mirror-Breaker to control the early game and close out with an overwhelming clock.

Archetype: Midrange-Combo
Budget: ~$450
Key Cards: Archfiend of the Dross, Unholy Annex, Fable of the Mirror-Breaker, Bloodtithe Harvester
