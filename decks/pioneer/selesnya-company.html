Selesnya Company

Tier 1 13.5% of meta 53% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

Selesnya Company is a midrange deck that uses efficient mana creatures to power into Collected Company for value. The deck combines aggressive creatures like Voice of Resurgence and Tarmogoyf with disruptive elements like Archon of Emeria and Thalia, Guardian of Thraben to control the game's pace while developing a board state that overwhelms opponents through superior card quality and creature synergies.

Archetype: Midrange
Budget: ~$680
Key Cards: Collected Company, Voice of Resurgence, Tarmogoyf, Noble Hierarch
