Izzet Prowess

Tier 1 12% of meta 55% win rate Intermediate

Deck Overview

Izzet Prowess is a fast, spellslinging aggro-combo deck that leverages low-cost creatures with prowess and cheap instants/sorceries to overwhelm opponents. It uses card draw like Expressive Iteration and Consider to refill hand while burning the opponent with Lightning Bolt and Searing Blaze. The deck can switch to a control role with Spell Queller and counterspells, making it highly adaptable in the Pioneer meta.

Archetype: Aggro-Combo
Budget: ~$250
Key Cards: Stormchaser Mage, Crackling Drake, Fathom Fleet Cutlass, Lightning Bolt, Spell Queller
